Privacy Policy

Plain-English draft, effective July 2026. Subject to solicitor review before commercial launch.

What we collect

Account details you give us (name, email, date of birth, a hashed password), your entries and play-credit ledger, and messages you send via the Support centre. We do not collect payment details in test mode.

Why

To run your account and entries, verify you are 18+, credit prizes, answer support messages, and keep provable records of draws for fairness verification. Legal bases: performing our contract with you, and legitimate interests in running verifiable competitions.

Where it lives

Data is stored with Supabase on infrastructure in the EU, behind access controls; the site is served by Vercel. We do not sell your data, run third-party advertising, or send marketing without asking first.

How long

Account and entry records are kept while your account is open and for as long as needed to evidence competition results, then deleted or anonymised.

Your rights

You can ask for a copy of your data, correction, or deletion at any time via the Support centre. You can complain to the ICO (ico.org.uk) if you're unhappy with how we handle data.

Contact

IGX Ltd, via the Support centre on this site.

InstantWins.co.uk is operated by IGX Ltd. All draws form a single continuous arrangement of free-entry prize competitions under Schedule 2 of the Gambling Act 2005. Test mode: all credit is play-money. 18+, UK residents only. Structure subject to solicitor sign-off before launch.
