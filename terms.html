Terms & Conditions

Plain-English draft, effective July 2026. Subject to solicitor review before commercial launch.

1. Who we are

InstantWins.co.uk is operated by IGX Ltd ("we"), the promoter of all competitions on this site.

2. What this site is

All draws and games on this site form a single continuous arrangement of prize competitions with a free entry route, structured under the free-entry provisions of Schedule 2 of the Gambling Act 2005. They are not gambling and no gambling licence is required. The games on screen (wheels, balls, cards, dice) are presentation only: every entry's outcome is pre-set by its numbered position in a sealed allocation fixed before entries open, and nothing you do in a game changes it.

3. Test mode

The site currently operates in test mode. All credit is play-money with no cash value; no payments are taken and no cash prizes are paid. These terms will be updated, and re-accepted by you, before any real-money operation begins.

4. Eligibility

You must be 18 or over and a UK resident. One account per person. We may suspend accounts we reasonably believe breach these terms.

5. Entries, pools and draws

Instant games run in numbered pools with a fixed entry cap and fixed prize table published per pool; a pool's allocation is committed (SHA-256) before it opens and its seed is revealed when the final entry sells. Any pool still open 28 days after opening settles then, with unsold-entry prizes drawn among that pool's entrants. Card draws run to a published schedule; winners are derived from the sealed seed and final entry count. Entries are non-refundable once revealed or once a draw is off.

6. Free entry

Every pool and draw can be entered free by post with identical odds; free entries are processed ahead of paid sales. See the Free entry page. No purchase necessary.

7. Fair play

Automated play, multiple accounts, or exploiting technical faults may lead to entries being voided. Obvious errors (e.g. a mispriced pool) may be corrected, with affected entries refunded to credit.

8. Liability

Nothing limits liability that cannot lawfully be limited. Otherwise our liability to you is limited to re-crediting affected entries. The site is provided "as is" during test mode.

Contact us via the Support centre. We may update these terms; material changes will be announced on the site.
