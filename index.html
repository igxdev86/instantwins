The outcome is never in the game

Every prize is locked in before anyone buys — the full allocation is fixed and its fingerprint published on the game page before a batch opens. The ball, the claw, the rocket are presentation only — they reveal the prize already attached to your entry number and change nothing.

Verify every single pool

The moment a pool sells out, its seed is revealed. Re-hash it yourself and confirm it matches the commitment shown while the pool was live. Any pool still open after 28 days settles then, prizes drawn among its entrants. Full detail on the Fairness page.
