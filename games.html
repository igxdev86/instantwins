How the games work

The outcome is never in the game. Before each pool opens, a shuffled entry→prize allocation is fixed and its SHA-256 hash is published on the game page. Buying an entry reveals the prize already attached to your entry number — the plinko ball, the claw, the rocket are presentation only and change nothing.

The seed is revealed at sell-out. The moment a pool's final entry sells, its seed is published so anyone can re-hash it and confirm it matches the commitment shown while the pool was live. Any pool still open 28 days after opening settles then, with unsold-entry prizes drawn among its entrants.

Free entry, always. Every pool of every game can be entered free by post with identical odds — free entries are processed ahead of paid sales. See the Free entry page.

All games form part of the single continuous arrangement of free-entry prize draws on InstantWins.co.uk, structured under the free-entry provisions of Schedule 2 of the Gambling Act 2005.
